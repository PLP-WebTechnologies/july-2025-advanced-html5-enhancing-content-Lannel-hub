About Kenya 🇰🇪

  • Kenya is located in East Africa, bordered by the Indian Ocean
  • Capital city: Nairobi (known as the "Green City in the Sun")
  • Famous for the Great Migration in Maasai Mara
  • Home to Mount Kenya, the second-highest mountain in Africa
  • Birthplace of many world-renowned long-distance runners
  • M-Pesa mobile money system originated here
